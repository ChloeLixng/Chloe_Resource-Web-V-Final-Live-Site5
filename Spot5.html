Store Description

Two sisters on a mission to bring Korean comforts to the Toronto community through delicious drinks and baked goods, while providing a homey and comfortable space for the community to come read, work and connect with others. One of our biggest challenges growing up Canadian-Korean from a young age was us always trying to figure out where we ‘truly belonged’. On one hand, you are trying to fit in at a school and with friends that look different from you, eat different from you and have different family situations. On the other hand, you have parents that love you and want you to grow up the way they did. So they raise you the way they know how. Constantly being between two worlds was often isolating and lonely. That is why we started forget me not coffee. To build a genuine place where everyone belongs and is not forgotten. To drink delicious drinks and share delicious snacks. We all deserve to feel like we belong. - FORGET ME NOT COFFEE
