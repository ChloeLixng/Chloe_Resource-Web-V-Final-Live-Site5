Store Description

Starting as an 18-foot coffee truck, we've transformed into a charming cafe named '18feet' to honor our roots. At 18feet, we create exceptional K-drinks and desserts that merge traditional flavors with contemporary style. Each item features high-quality ingredients and innovative recipes, making us a standout in Toronto's culinary landscape. We focus on using fresh, locally sourced ingredients and produce our own Cheong, Dalgona, and cream cheese with the finest materials available. Relish generous servings of our outstanding K-drinks and desserts at affordable prices, ensuring that indulgence is within everyone's reach. Our team is dedicated to crafting unique flavors and a varied menu, continually refreshing our offerings as leaders in the beverage and dessert sector. We curate our café experience around our customers, leveraging trend analysis and feedback to deliver delightful K-drinks and desserts that align with changing preferences. - 18 FEET
