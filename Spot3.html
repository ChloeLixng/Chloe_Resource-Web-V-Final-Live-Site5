Store Description

At Matcha Haus, we’re passionate about bringing people together through authentic, high-quality matcha. Every drink we craft starts with first-harvest matcha, sourced directly from a collective of small generational farms in Nagasaki, Japan. Our leaves are carefully shade-grown and harvested just once a year- ensuring a richer flavor and smoother texture. - MATCHA HAUS
